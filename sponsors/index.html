The anti-conflict guarantee

The Tribe Council — six elected members of the Guild — votes on every inbound sponsor before we accept the deal. If they reject you, we don't take your money.

That's the design. Sponsors who get accepted are sponsors the audience already wants in the room. Members aren't being sold to; they helped pick the brands they want around them.

Result: higher conversion than a typical sponsor placement, and zero tone risk to your brand.

How it works

From inquiry to live placement: about 14 days. Everything is documented and signed before any money moves.

Apply

Fill out the sponsorship inquiry form. Tell us about your brand, your audience, what you'd want out of a season. Takes about 5 minutes.

Discovery call

30 minutes with the Guild ops team. We talk through which tier fits, which theme month aligns with your launch calendar, what success looks like.

Council vote

Your application goes to the Tribe Council for an async 7-day vote. If 4 of 6 approve, we move forward.

Contract + payment

One-month sponsorship agreement, signed via DocuSign. Payment via Stripe invoice. You only pay after the council approves.

Onboarding

Logo, copy, trailer assets, offer details collected. We build out your placements and loop you in for review before they go live.

Live season

Day 1 theme drop with your branding. The season runs for 28 days. You get a live channel inside the Guild for real-time questions during the month.

Recap report

Day 30: a full report. Impressions, clicks, member sentiment quotes, top entries, winners, performance vs benchmarks. Honest numbers.

FAQ

Why is this different from a typical sponsorship?
Most sponsorships are placement-based: your logo lives somewhere on a creator's content for a month, and you hope the audience cares. Builders Marketplace sponsorships are structurally embedded: members are competing for a prize you're funding. That changes the dynamic from "sponsor to be ignored" to "sponsor to be remembered." Plus, the Tribe Council vote means the audience is pre-disposed to like you before you ever show up.
What kinds of brands do well here?
Tools, platforms, services, and educational products that genuinely help side-hustle builders ship work. Notion templates, AI tools, design software, hosting platforms, payment processors, course platforms, accounting tools, freelance marketplaces. Brands targeting "ambitious people building income" do well. Brands targeting "consumers who want to spend money on lifestyle goods" don't fit.
Can I pick which month I sponsor?
Yes. The 12-month theme calendar is locked, so you can match a sponsorship to a theme that fits your audience. Reach out at least 60 days before the month you're targeting; some popular themes book out.
What does the Tribe Council vote actually evaluate?
Three things: (1) is the brand a fit for the audience, (2) is there a real offer or value for members beyond a logo, (3) is there any conflict with the Guild's mission. Brands rarely get rejected if they're a clean fit. The vote exists more to protect the Guild's tone than to gate-keep aggressively.
Do you guarantee a number of impressions or conversions?
No. We share honest historic benchmarks and projected metrics, but we don't guarantee outcomes — that's how creator-economy sponsorships are run by people you should not trust. We DO guarantee deliverables (logo placement, trailer mention, sponsor channel, judging seat at the relevant tiers) and we DO send a full recap with actual numbers within 7 days of season close.
What happens to the sponsor revenue?
Local Sponsor: 100% to the Guild (covers contest costs + admin). Theme Sponsor: 90% Guild, 10% to a profit pool split among the 6-member Tribe Council. Title Sponsor: 80% Guild, 20% to the Council pool. The Council members are explicitly aligned with you signing — they earn a small share of what you pay. That's intentional alignment.
Is there an exclusivity option?
Title Sponsor includes right of first refusal on the same theme month next year. Within a single season, only one Title Sponsor and at most two Theme Sponsors are accepted. Local Sponsor can have up to four per month.
Who do I talk to?
The signup form routes to the Guild ops team. We respond within 24 hours on weekdays.
