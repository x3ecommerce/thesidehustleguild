About · How The Guild Runs

Built by a team. Powered by a Council.

The Guild is operated by a small ops team and a rotating Council of outside experts. The community pays the community. Here's how it actually works.

The model in one sentence

The Side Hustle Guild is a paid Discord community where 25% of every subscription dollar becomes the monthly cash prize pool. Members submit Hustle Cards — real things they've built. A rotating Council of outside experts judges them. The top three each month split the pool.

The community pays the community. No middleman skimming. The prize-pool math is public and updated live at thesidehustleguild.com/submissions.

💰 How money flows

Every $1 a member pays splits like this:

  • $0.25 → monthly prize pool (paid out to top-3 contest winners)
  • $0.30 → affiliate commissions (members who refer paying members earn 30% recurring)
  • $0.05 → payment processing (Whop + Stripe)
  • $0.40 → operations (the team, the agents, the infrastructure, the Council expert fees)

The Council

Each month, a different outside expert joins as our Council Session host. They bring their specific framework to one Wednesday voice session. Members bring their stuck things. The Council expert delivers.

Different topics rotate: pricing, productized services, content growth, paid ads, AI workflows, hiring your first contractor, when to quit a side project. We pay the Council expert directly. Their reputation gets exposure to our audience. Members get specific tactical answers, not generic advice.

Want a deep look at your own work? The Hustle Audit ($99) is a written 1-2 page agent-generated deep-dive on your submission — positioning, pricing, and execution. Delivered within 24 hours. 25% of every audit funds the prize pool.

What you're paying for

The honest framing: The Guild is not a course, not a guru funnel, not a mastermind. It's a paid Discord where:

  • The contest gives you a real reason to ship every month
  • The rituals (Monday Drop, Wednesday Council, Friday Wins, Sunday Reset) keep you in motion
  • The room is full of people building real things — not lurkers looking for shortcuts
  • The Council brings outside expertise on rotation so you're not just hearing one voice
  • Your Hustle Card submissions get archived publicly so other builders can learn from you

You're paying $9-$19 a month for the structure and the room. The contest pool is the bonus that scales as the community grows.

Who runs The Guild

The Guild is operated under X3 E-Commerce LLC dba The Side Hustle Guild, based in Boulder, Colorado. The ops team handles infrastructure, partnerships, payouts, and moderation. The Council is recruited monthly. The day-to-day public-facing work is done by the agent team.

We deliberately keep individual names off public surfaces. The reason: The Guild belongs to its members. The brand is the room you're paying to be in, not the personality of any one operator. That's true today, it'll be true if the ops team grows, and it'll be true a decade from now.
