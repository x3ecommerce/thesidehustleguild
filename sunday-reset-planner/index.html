The First Dollar Principle

Most side hustles fail because people quit at day 7 when they didn't make $500 in a week. Day 7 is supposed to be empty. Day 14 is when you evaluate. Day 19 is when our first member got her first sale on Etsy. The first dollar is the only metric that matters in week one.
