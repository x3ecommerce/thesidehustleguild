Frequently asked

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your Whop dashboard at any time. Refunds within 7 days of initial purchase, no questions asked. Pauses also available — pause preserves your founder rate and contest history.

What if I joined as Founder ($9), can I upgrade to Insider later?

Yes. You can add the Insider tier ($39/mo) without losing your locked Founder rate. Your $9 base stays the same; Insider becomes an additional $30/mo on top. Your gold founder role + your gold insider role both display.

What's a Hustle Audit and why does it cost $99 in a $19 community?

The Hustle Audit is a 10-page written deep-dive on your specific submission — positioning, pricing, market analysis, execution roadmap, distribution strategy. It's generated by five analyst personas working in parallel against your data. Most consulting at this depth runs $1,500-3,000 from human consultants. We can do it for $99 because the team that delivers it is the agent fleet, not humans on hourly rates. 25% of every audit funds the prize pool.

How does Concierge Pro differ from the regular weekly rituals?

The weekly rituals (Monday Drop, Wednesday Council Session, etc.) are public posts everyone in Discord sees. Concierge Pro is private 1:1 coaching via slash commands — Claude trained on your specific member profile + the SHG playbook, on-demand, unlimited. Most members use it 2-4 times a week for quick gut checks.

Can I be on Insider without first being a Founder/Standard member?

No. Insider is an upgrade tier — it requires an active base membership ($9 or $19). The Insider $39 is the upgrade fee, not a replacement.

What if Insider's 50 seats are full?

You join the waitlist. When someone churns, the next person on the waitlist gets the open seat at the rate locked at the time they signed up for the waitlist. We hold the seat for 48 hours; if you don't claim it, it passes to the next person.

Are you a course? A coaching program? A mastermind?

No. The Side Hustle Guild is a paid Discord community with a built-in monthly cash contest. We're explicitly not a course (no curriculum), not coaching (no one calls you), not a mastermind (no group calls beyond the public Council Sessions). You pay for structure, room access, peer network, and the contest pool.

Who runs The Guild?

The Guild is operated under X3 E-Commerce LLC dba The Side Hustle Guild, based in Boulder, Colorado. Day-to-day operations run via a small team plus a fleet of specialized agents we built in-house. We keep individual names off public surfaces — the brand is the room, not any one operator. See /about/council for the full team structure.
