What is this?

This is the public face of the Builders Marketplace, the monthly skill-based contest that runs inside The Side Hustle Guild. Every card here was submitted by a member of the Guild. The contest awards cash prizes per tier so a Rookie competing on their first dollar isn't matched against an Operator running a small machine. Same room, separate ladders.

You'll see Hustle Cards from real builders. Real numbers, real proof, real talk. We keep the threads in Discord because that's where the conversation actually happens. This page is the index.
