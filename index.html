Frequently asked

Real questions, straight answers.

How does the prize pool work?

Each month, 25% of subscription gross revenue becomes the cash pool. The Prize Pool Calculator agent computes this daily and posts the snapshot in #leaderboards. The contest activates automatically the moment the 100th paid member joins. Pool scales as the community grows.

What if my hustle is brand new?

Submit anyway. We have a Rookie tier specifically for first-time builders. Day-one numbers are valid numbers. Zero in sales is a number. Honest entries beat hype every time.

Why $9 for Founders if it costs you to run?

50 charter members at $9/mo = $450/mo recurring. That funds infrastructure indefinitely. After 50, standard tier is $19. We're trading discounted lifetime price for the founding members who help build the room from the floor.

What happens at member 100?

The Subscriber Counter agent fires automatically: Theme Drop announcement, submission window opens for 10 days, leaderboards go live, email blast to the newsletter, sponsor track activates. None of that is manual — it's wired.

Is this gambling?

No. The contest is a skill-based competition judged on documented entries. We don't sell raffle tickets. Submissions are scored on clarity, proof, trajectory, fit-to-theme, and originality by a designated judging panel.

Can I cancel?

Yes — Lab Member is month-to-month, cancel anytime via Whop. Founder is also cancelable but you lose the lifetime $9 rate if you do. No contracts, no commitments, no shame for leaving.
